The Kinetiq Story

Founded by visionary designer Samuel Pacris II, Kinetiq was born from a frustration with the status quo. The footwear industry forced a choice: pure performance or lifestyle aesthetic. Samuel believed you shouldn't have to choose.

Our mission is simple: To engineer the most technologically advanced footwear on the planet, wrapped in an unapologetic, futuristic design language. We exist at the intersection of extreme athletic performance and raw street culture.
